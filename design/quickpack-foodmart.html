Quick Pack Food Mart

Quick Pack Food Mart is one of the best fried chicken joints in Seattle's Central District. At the time of writing, this pseudo-gas station has 4.7 stars from 86 reviews.

Despite all of this popularity, the businesses branding definitely leaves a lot to be desired. As a personal project, I took it upon myself to reimagine what a renewed brand image could look like for this business.

Original Design

This is what the logo looks like today.

New Logo

The new logo aims to highlight key information about the business quickly.

Tagline

I chose to keep Quick Pack's existing tagline in place, since I think it does a good job at highlighting their #1 selling item. However, I did use color to emphasize that this is the BEST fried chicken, hoping to entice new customers.

Alternative Logo

A different branding style that can be used with a combination of icons.

Store Banner

The store has a very thin banner that sits above the main entrance. To round out this brand package, I felt that there would need to be a new banner for the store. I kept much of the original information, but decided to try and give more emphasis to the tagline. I also added in the chicken leg icon to give readers more context about the business at a glance.
