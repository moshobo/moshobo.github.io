Track Maps

Description: In 2021, I saw some really creative maps of racing circuits on Reddit. Every other comment on the post was “Where can I get one of these?!”.

I too was curious where I could get one.

Many months went by, the author kept sharing more and more designs, but they were never available for purchase. I wanted one on my wall! As the racing season came to an end and with no answer was in sight, I decided to look into designing my own posters.

The outcome is what you see below. I created my brand, Moshobo Makes, and now share my various track designs with the motorsport community.
